Who We Are

Avenged Sevenfold (abbreviated as A7X) is our American heavy metal band from Huntington Beach, California, formed in 1999. Our band's current lineup consists of vocalist M. Shadows, rhythm guitarist Zacky Vengeance, lead guitarist Synyster Gates, bassist Johnny Christ, and drummer Brooks Wackerman.
